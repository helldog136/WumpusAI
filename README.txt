# WumpusAI
Custom (simple) Wumpus game and AI

Rules of wumpus:
  You are exploring a cave of unknown dimension in search of a treasure.
  There is a Wumpus hidden in the cave wich makes the area around it smelly.
  There are Pits which make wind.
  Pits and Wumpus are deadly
  You have to get out with the gold to win
  You can shoot arrow(s) (by default 3) to kill the wumpus
